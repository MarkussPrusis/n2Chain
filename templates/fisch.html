Fisch

Fisch is a fishing and exploration experience created by WoozyNate. It was officially released on October 5, 2024 and quickly gained widespread popularity on Roblox. Fisch was originally published by WoozyNate himself, but transferred to the Fisching group on November 4, 2024 after its acquisition. Players can begin fishing by equipping their fishing rod and standing near water. The bobber can be cast by holding down left-click and then releasing. 'Shake' buttons will appear on the screen, which can be optionally clicked to speed up the catching process. Once a fish bites, a minigame starts in which the player must hold or release left-click to keep the bar inside the moving white rectangle. As the bar stays inside, a progress bar fills up. Once the progress bar is full, the fish is successfully caught. Every caught fish is cataloged in the player's Bestiary. Fish can otherwise be sold to merchants for C$ - the main currency, which can be used to buy items such as fishing rods, bait, and boats. Every fish has a range of possible weights and a small chance of having a mutation, which increases their value and alters their appearance. Players can use boats to explore other islands and discover new fish.
